# TOEFL iBT Exam Configuration

## Test Format

| Section | Duration | Questions | Score |
|---------|----------|-----------|-------|
| Reading | 35 min | 20 questions (2 passages) | 0-30 |
| Listening | 36 min | 28 questions (3 lectures, 2 conversations) | 0-30 |
| Speaking | 16 min | 4 tasks | 0-30 |
| Writing | 29 min | 2 tasks | 0-30 |
| **Total** | ~2 hours | — | **0-120** |

## Section Details

### Reading (35 minutes)
- 2 academic passages (~700 words each)
- 10 questions per passage
- Question types: vocabulary, inference, factual, insert text, prose summary

### Listening (36 minutes)
- 3 lectures (6 questions each)
- 2 conversations (5 questions each)
- Question types: main idea, detail, inference, attitude, organization

### Speaking (16 minutes)
| Task | Type | Prep | Response |
|------|------|------|----------|
| 1 | Independent (opinion) | 15 sec | 45 sec |
| 2 | Integrated (campus) | 30 sec | 60 sec |
| 3 | Integrated (academic) | 30 sec | 60 sec |
| 4 | Integrated (lecture) | 20 sec | 60 sec |

### Writing (29 minutes)
| Task | Type | Time |
|------|------|------|
| Integrated | Read + Listen + Write | 20 min |
| Academic Discussion | Forum post response | 10 min |

## Score Interpretation

| Score Range | Level | Description |
|-------------|-------|-------------|
| 0-42 | Below Basic | Minimal English proficiency |
| 43-71 | Low Intermediate | Basic communication skills |
| 72-94 | High Intermediate | Functional academic English |
| 95-113 | Advanced | Strong academic English |
| 114-120 | Superior | Near-native proficiency |

## Key Dates & Logistics

- **Registration**: Opens 6 months before test date
- **Score release**: 4-8 days after test
- **Score validity**: 2 years from test date
- **Score sends**: $20 per report (6-10 days delivery)
- **Reschedule fee**: $60 (4+ days before) / Full fee (less than 4 days)
- **Cancel fee**: $0 (get partial refund) / varies by timing

## MyBest Scores

ETS automatically calculates "MyBest" scores combining best section scores across all tests in past 2 years.

**University policies vary:**
- Some accept MyBest (check specific program)
- Many require single-sitting scores
- Some accept either (MyBest gives flexibility)

## Test Versions

| Version | Where | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| TOEFL iBT | Test center | Standard, most accepted |
| TOEFL iBT Home Edition | Home | Same format, online proctored |
| TOEFL Essentials | Home | Shorter, adaptive, less accepted |
| TOEFL Paper Edition | Limited locations | Being phased out |

Most applications refer to "TOEFL iBT" — verify which versions your target institutions accept.
