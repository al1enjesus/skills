# Sovereignty Scoring Methodology

## Score Range: 0-100

Score each data point on these dimensions, then average:

### Alliance Strength (0-25)
- 25: New defense pacts, joint military exercises, trade agreements
- 15: Reaffirmed commitments, diplomatic meetings
- 5: Tensions, disagreements, withdrawn commitments
- 0: Alliance breakdowns, sanctions between allies

### Industrial Sovereignty (0-25)
- 25: Reshoring manufacturing, new domestic production, supply chain independence
- 15: Industrial policy announcements, subsidies for domestic production
- 5: Continued offshoring, dependency deepening
- 0: Critical supply chain failures, foreign acquisition of key industries

### Border & Migration Control (0-25)
- 25: Effective border policies, controlled immigration, integration success
- 15: Policy proposals, enforcement improvements
- 5: Uncontrolled flows, policy paralysis
- 0: Border crises, mass irregular migration

### Cultural & Institutional Resilience (0-25)
- 25: Strong civic institutions, cultural confidence, educational excellence
- 15: Reform initiatives, institutional improvements
- 5: Institutional decay, cultural fragmentation
- 0: Systemic institutional failure

## Aggregate Score
- Average all applicable dimensions
- Weight recent events (last 7 days) at 1.5x
- Round to nearest integer
